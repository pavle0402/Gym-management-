Welcome to Our Gym

Gym Rules

  1. No eating or drinking allowed inside the gym.
  2. Wear appropriate workout attire and clean gym shoes at all times.
  3. Always use a towel when using gym equipment.
  4. Put back all equipment and weights to their designated places after use.
  5. Respect other members and gym staff. Use appropriate language and behavior.
  6. Follow the instructions of gym staff for your safety.
  7. Refrain from using mobile phones in the workout area.
  8. Use lockers to store your belongings and bring your lock.
  9. Notify staff immediately of any broken equipment or safety hazards.
  10. Keep noise levels to a minimum, especially in designated quiet areas.

Gym Policies

  • Membership fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Gym opening hours may vary and will be posted in advance.
  • Personal training sessions require advance booking and have additional fees.
  • Children under the age of 16 must be accompanied by an adult at all times.
  • Smoking and alcohol consumption are prohibited within the gym premises.
  • Gym management is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
  • Any damage caused to gym property due to negligence will be charged to the member.
  • Members are responsible for their own health and should seek medical advice before starting any exercise program.
  • Gym staff reserve the right to deny access to anyone violating the rules or displaying unsafe behavior.

Staff Guidelines

  1. Greet every member with a smile and offer assistance when needed.
  2. Be knowledgeable about gym facilities, services, and class schedules.
  3. Keep the gym clean and organized at all times.
  4. Ensure all gym equipment is in proper working condition and report any issues to management promptly.
  5. Be attentive to members' safety during workouts and provide guidance when necessary.
  6. Maintain a professional and respectful demeanor towards all members and colleagues.
  7. Handle member inquiries and complaints with patience and empathy, escalating issues to management when required.
  8. Adhere to the gym's dress code and wear your staff badge at all times.
  9. Participate in ongoing training and professional development to enhance your skills.
  10. Respect member privacy and confidentiality regarding personal information.
  11. Conduct yourself in a manner that reflects positively on the gym's reputation.

Code of Conduct

As a staff member of our gym, you are expected to adhere to the following code of conduct:

  1. Be honest, trustworthy, and act with integrity in all your interactions.
  2. Respect the diversity and inclusivity of our gym community.
  3. Avoid conflicts of interest and never accept gifts or favors from members or vendors.
  4. Refrain from using your position for personal gain or unauthorized purposes.
  5. Do not disclose confidential or sensitive information about the gym or its members.
  6. Report any unethical behavior or policy violations to management immediately.
  7. Follow all local laws and regulations related to gym operations.
